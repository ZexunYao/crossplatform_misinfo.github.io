Description of the project

Objective/Motivation:

Identify and characterize the cross-platform diffusion and proliferation of harmful content and misinformation


General Goals of the research:

  • Investigate how harmful narratives flow across diverse platforms
  • predict which stories will gain traction on mainstream media
  • uncover malicious activity to spread misinformation in low- and high-moderated platforms

Study 1: Pathways to Virality

  • Task: identify intra- and cross-platform diffusion pathways of viral content
  • Study case: diffusion of links (URLs) within and across platforms
  • Goals:
      1. identify and characterize the URLs that got viral on multiple platforms
      2. examine the users interaction patterns (intra-platform) that brought a URL to get viral
      3. investigate the diffusion of viral URLs across platforms and their growth in virality over time

Deliverables study 1

  • Deliverable 1.1: The diffusion of shared URLs and interaction network among multiple platforms
  • Deliverable 1.2: The diffusion of viral URLs across platforms and their growth and relation over time
  • Deliverable 1.3: The diffusion, contents and temporal evolution of Google Docs and Google Forms

Study 2: Moderated posts detection

  • Task: design an AI approach to automatically detect the diffusion of moderated content on different platforms
  • Study case: screenshots of removed Twitter posts are often shared on Twitter and other platforms
  • Goals:
      1. detect and study the content of these screenshots
      2. identify and characterize the authors of the original post and the spreaders of screenshots
      3. investigate the diffusion of screenshots across multiple platforms

Deliverables of Study 2

  • Deliverable 2.1: Download and create images
  • Deliverable 2.2: Build a supervised learning model using CNN
  • Deliverable 2.3: Build an unsupervised learning model
